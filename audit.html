AI Audit

A focused, two-hour workshop designed to evaluate your organization's AI maturity, explore high-value use cases, and provide a preliminary roadmap for AI adoption, without requiring extensive preparation or internal resources.

Deliverables

1

Pre-Session Discovery Questionnaire

Before the call, the client receives a short readiness form to complete (10 - 15 questions). This gathers context on:

  • Business priorities and digital transformation goals
  • Current data and system infrastructure
  • Organizational skill levels and team capabilities
  • Existing AI or automation efforts
2

Two-Hour Live AI Audit Call

Led by a Second Foundation AI data scientist, this structured discussion covers:

Hour 1: Readiness Review

  • Assess data environment, technology stack, and processes
  • Discuss current pain points and business challenges
  • Score organizational readiness using Second Foundation AI's maturity model

Hour 2: Opportunity Identification

  • Brainstorm potential AI applications tied to business outcomes
  • Prioritize 2 - 3 initiatives by impact vs. feasibility
  • Identify next steps for proof-of-concept or pilot projects
3

Post-Session Summary Report

Within 3 - 5 business days, the client receives a concise deliverables package including:

  • AI Readiness Snapshot: Numeric score with supporting commentary
  • Top 3 AI Opportunities: Description, potential business impact, estimated complexity
  • Preliminary Roadmap: 90-day action plan and next steps for pilot exploration
  • Recommended Implementation Partners: Introductions to vetted AI solution providers (optional)
4

Follow-Up Consultation

A 30-minute follow-up call with the data scientist or strategy consultant to:

  • Review the summary
  • Refine use case selection
  • Discuss partnership or project scoping options
