Case Study

Enhancing digital assistant TOBi with generative AI

Vodafone used IBM watsonx technologies to accelerate TOBi conversational journey creation, testing, and optimization across markets and languages.

Challenge

  • TOBi serves customers 24x7 in approximately 14 languages, creating significant scale and content demands.
  • Vodafone wanted to improve how quickly conversational journeys could be built and tested.
  • Gap analysis between the Central Library and local market journeys lacked practical execution capacity.
  • Teams needed better ways to evaluate sentiment and journey quality before exposing flows to live traffic.

Solution

  • IBM-led workshops identified five priority generative AI use cases, then focused on journey testing and gap analysis.
  • IBM Client Engineering and IBM Consulting delivered an accelerated five-week pilot build.
  • An orchestration service on IBM Cloud Code Engine executed multiple Voiceflow testing scenarios.
  • IBM watsonx.ai simulated customer interactions with TOBi and generated transcripts for deep analysis.
  • watsonx.ai was used for sentiment and emotional analysis, plus comparison across Central Library and market journeys.

Results

  • 99% improvement in journey testing turnaround time.
  • Testing dropped from around 6.5 hours per journey to less than one minute per journey per persona.
  • Gap analysis became feasible, with comprehensive analysis in less than five minutes per journey.
  • Pilot established a path to time savings and stronger pre-production quality control.
  • Improved sentiment visibility is expected to increase customer satisfaction through better journey readiness.
